Chemical Elements

The periodic table is a tabular arrangment of chemical elements Each element is represented by its chemical symbol, such as Fe for iron, H for Hydrogen, and O for oxygen. Elements groups and periodic based on their properties.

Chemical Equations

Chemical reactions are represented using chemical equations. Here's an example of a chemical reaction:

2H2 + O2 → 2H2 0

This equation represents the reaction between hydrogen gas (H2)and oxygen gas (O2)to form water (H2O).
